SELF-HOSTING THE FONTS  (2 manual downloads — ~2 minutes)
=========================================================
The site now loads fonts from static/fonts/ instead of the Fontshare CDN.
Until you add the two files below, browsers fall back to system fonts
(the site still looks fine — just not the exact branded typefaces).

1) Clash Display
   - Go to: https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/clash-display
   - Click "Download Family" (top right)
   - Unzip. In the unzipped folder open: Fonts/Variable/
   - Copy  ClashDisplay-Variable.woff2  into  static\fonts\

2) Satoshi
   - Go to: https://www.fontshare.com/fonts/satoshi
   - Click "Download Family"
   - Unzip. Open: Fonts/Variable/
   - Copy  Satoshi-Variable.woff2  into  static\fonts\

3) Delete PLACEHOLDER.txt (optional), commit, push.

If a download only gives .ttf/.otf, convert to woff2 at
https://cloudconvert.com/ttf-to-woff2  (or keep the link version).
The @font-face rules expect those exact filenames — keep the names as-is.
